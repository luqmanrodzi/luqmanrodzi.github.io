    Easy Skate school was started by Amin and Luqman. The idea was come when see there is no platform for people to learn a skateboard safely. Skateboarding is one of dangerous sport, so when people want to learn they will be got hurt.

    We believe when people start learn a skateboard with us, they can avoid from injury and can get a support to learn a basic trick to start a skateboard.
